Why are commission screenshots easy to overtrust?

A cropped image can show numbers without explaining whether they belong to a referral ledger, a bonus dashboard, a demo panel, or a temporary campaign view. Without the account path and terms wording, the screenshot stays weak evidence.

Evidence to keep before the next step

Save the full screenshot, any visible code or referral ID, the page path, and the terms wording connected to that commission claim. If only the balance area is visible, record that limitation clearly.

FAQ

Can a K1 Game commission screenshot prove real earnings?

Not by itself. It should be matched with terms, ledger context, and a visible page route.

Why are cropped screenshots weak?

They hide the account path, page labels, and whether the value is withdrawable or only promotional.

What proof improves trust?

A full screenshot, referral ID or code, related terms wording, and the visible ledger context improve trust.
